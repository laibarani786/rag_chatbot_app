GlobalMart is an online shopping platform launched in 2018. 
It sells electronics, clothing, books, and household items.

GlobalMart has a return policy of 30 days for all products.

GlobalMart offers free shipping on orders above $50.

The customer support team is available 24/7 via chat and email.

Payments can be made through credit card, debit card, PayPal, or cash on delivery.

GlobalMart operates in more than 25 countries worldwide.

The company launched its mobile app in 2020.

GlobalMart provides exclusive membership discounts to its premium members.
